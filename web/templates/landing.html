What is this

A simulation of the inner self

Mind Simulation is an experimental project inspired by the Internal Family Systems (IFS) model, which views the mind as a collection of distinct "parts" — each with its own emotions, motivations, and perspective.

Instead of a single AI response, the system creates an internal debate between multiple personalities you define. They may agree, disagree, challenge each other, or form alliances before producing a final answer.

How it works

The inner debate

1

Define your personalities

Name and describe your inner voices. Use the defaults or create your own — the Protector, the Dreamer, the Critic.

2

Send a message

Each personality reads your message and takes a position. They speak to each other, reveal feelings, and propose answers.

3

The judge decides

A neutral judge watches the debate unfold and declares a winner when one voice clearly takes control.

4

The winner speaks

The dominant personality delivers the final reply in its own natural voice. You see the full internal debate before the answer.

5

Talk to one directly

Prefix your message with @Name to bypass the debate and speak to a specific personality one-on-one. Click any name in the debate to address them.
